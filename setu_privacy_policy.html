Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 21, 2025

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Setu AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our blog-to-Pinterest automation service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and account credentials
  • Blog URLs: URLs of blog posts you want to convert to Pinterest pins
  • Pinterest Account Data: We access your Pinterest account through OAuth 2.0 with your explicit permission

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens to interact with Pinterest API on your behalf
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service (number of pins created, features used)
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, and access times for security purposes

1.3 Information From Pinterest

  • User Profile: Basic Pinterest profile information (username, profile URL)
  • Board Information: List of your Pinterest boards to enable pin posting
  • Pin Confirmation: Confirmation that pins were successfully created

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our blog-to-Pinterest automation service
  • Process your blog content through AI to generate pin titles, descriptions, and hashtags
  • Post pins to your Pinterest account with your explicit approval
  • Authenticate your access to Pinterest via OAuth 2.0
  • Communicate with you about service updates and support
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Ensure compliance with Pinterest's policies and our Terms of Service
  • Monitor for abuse, spam, or violation of rate limits

3. Data Storage and Pinterest Compliance

3.1 What We Store

  • OAuth Access Tokens: Securely encrypted and stored to enable Pinterest posting
  • User Account Data: Email and basic account information
  • Generated Content: AI-generated pin titles, descriptions, and hashtags (only until approved/rejected)
  • Usage Statistics: Aggregated data about service usage for analytics

3.2 What We DO NOT Store

In compliance with Pinterest's Developer Guidelines:

  • We do NOT store Pinterest user data (boards, pins, followers, etc.)
  • We do NOT cache Pinterest API responses
  • We do NOT store your Pinterest profile information beyond the current session
  • All Pinterest data is retrieved fresh via API calls when needed

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

4.1 Pinterest

  • We use Pinterest API v5 to post pins on your behalf
  • Your data is subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy
  • We only request minimal OAuth scopes: pins:write and boards:read

4.2 AI Services

  • We use Claude AI (Anthropic) to analyze blog content and generate pin descriptions
  • Blog content is processed by AI but not permanently stored by the AI provider
  • AI-generated content is labeled as such in accordance with best practices

4.3 Hosting and Infrastructure

  • Our service is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (Vercel/Railway)
  • All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest and in transit
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure API communication with Pinterest

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data at any time
  • Edit: You can update your account information through your dashboard
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke Pinterest OAuth access at any time through Pinterest settings

6.2 Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your data in a portable format.

6.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting us.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Obligations: We may retain certain data longer if required by law
  • Generated Content: AI-generated pin content is deleted after posting or rejection

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification for material changes
